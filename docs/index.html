RVSC Viewer

VEConfigure settings reader · read-only

Your file never leaves your computer. Parsing happens entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Loaded-file history (below) is also kept only in this browser's local storage.

RVSC Viewer reads VEConfigure .rvsc settings files locally in your browser. It does not modify, save, or transmit files. Field mappings that have not been independently confirmed are shown as not yet mapped rather than guessed. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Victron Energy B.V. — "Victron", "VEConfigure" and related names are used here only to identify the file format this page reads.
